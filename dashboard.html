Clean management view of General, Life and early-stage Outsourcing using the supplied YTD April P&L, with shared IAS items allocated only to General and Life.

Visible assumptions and allocation rules

  • Shared income is split evenly between General and Life only; Outsourcing receives no shared income.
  • Jo adjustment transfers $60,000 of expense from Life to General.
  • Outsourcing allocation removes $50,000 from shared operating expenses before the remaining shared pool is split.
  • Outsourcing costs are salary only at $50,000 and no shared expense allocation is applied.
  • Forecast method: monthly average equals YTD divided by 10, then multiplied by 12.
  • Data cutoff is April, using the supplied raw P&L line items.
